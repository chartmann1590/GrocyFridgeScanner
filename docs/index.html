How It Works

Three taps to an updated inventory

No manual entry. No barcode scanning one item at a time. Just a photo and a tap.

1

Point & Shoot

Open the app and take a photo of a fridge shelf, a cupboard, or any food storage area.

2

AI Detects Everything

Gemma 4 E2B runs on your phone to identify each food item, count units, and determine container types — all without sending data anywhere.

3

Review & Sync

See every proposed change with before/after amounts. Edit names, adjust counts, or exclude items. Tap sync when you're happy.

4

Grocy Updated

Inventory is pushed directly to your self-hosted Grocy instance via its REST API. New products are created automatically if needed.

Features

Built for self-hosters who want simplicity

Every feature is designed around privacy, speed, and keeping your Grocy inventory accurate with minimal effort.

On-Device AI

Gemma 4 E2B runs entirely on your phone. No images are uploaded, no cloud APIs are called. Your kitchen photos stay private.

Smart Grouping

Duplicate detections are merged automatically. "chips", "Chips", and "chip" are recognized as the same product.

Review Everything

Every proposed change shows the delta. Toggle items on/off, edit product names, and adjust quantities before committing.

Full History

Every scan is recorded with a thumbnail, timestamp, and item-level breakdown. Filter by fridge or cupboards.

Auto Product Creation

Detected a food that doesn't exist in your Grocy yet? It's created automatically with the right unit and location.

Grocy Native

Built specifically for Grocy's API. Uses the inventory endpoint, respects locations, and matches quantity units.
